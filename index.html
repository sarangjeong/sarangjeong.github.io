J. Adolfo Hermosillo

About me

Hi! I am Adolfo, a second-year PhD student in the Department of Linguistics at Stanford advised by Christopher Potts. I am broadly interested in natural language processing, semantics, sociolinguistics and multilingualism. I use computational and experimental methods to answer questions about meaning and variation. I am a member of The Stanford NLP Group and ALPS.
